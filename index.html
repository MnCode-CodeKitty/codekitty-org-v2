Engineering skills are life skills.

Code Kitty teaches coding and robotics to learners grade 2 and up.

Proudly Non-Profit

We are a 501(c)(3) tax-exempt non-profit, funded by individual donations and public grants. The money we collect goes directly to building more robotics kits and offering more workshops.

Skills, not Sales

We are not a company selling a robot toy. We are an education non-profit whose goal is to educate and empower all learners through an integrated Coding and Robotics workshop experience.

Open for the Future

Our organization and our workshop experience are built on Open Source technology and Open Standards. All of our designs, materials and code are freely available on our site and elsewhere.

Code Kitty Pricing

Our pricing is based on the costs of hardware and production. All Code Kitty members are unpaid volunteers. A workshop is typically for 5-20 people.
